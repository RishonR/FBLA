Welcome to Overflux Studios

At Overflux Studios, we value our employees and strive to provide a supportive and rewarding work environment. Joining our team comes with a range of benefits aimed at enhancing your well-being and fostering a positive workplace culture.

Employee Benefits:

  • Competitive Salary Packages: We offer competitive salary packages to ensure that our employees are fairly compensated for their skills and contributions.
  • Health and Dental Insurance Coverage: Your health matters. Our comprehensive health and dental insurance plans provide coverage for medical and dental expenses.
  • 401(k) Retirement Savings Plan: Plan for your future with our 401(k) savings plan, designed to help you build financial security and achieve your long-term goals.
  • Paid Vacation and Holidays: Enjoy well-deserved time off with paid vacation days and holidays. We believe in the importance of rest and relaxation.
  • Flexible Work Hours and Remote Work Options: Achieve a healthy work-life balance with flexible work hours and the option to work remotely when needed.
  • Professional Development Opportunities: We invest in your growth. Access various professional development programs and resources to enhance your skills and advance your career.
  • Collaborative and Innovative Work Culture: Join a dynamic team and contribute to innovative projects in a collaborative work culture that values creativity and teamwork.

We believe in a healthy work-life balance and understand that each team member contributes uniquely to our success. With our comprehensive benefits package, we aim to support your professional growth and personal well-being.

Job Openings

Programmer

We are seeking a talented and experienced programmer to join our game development team. As a Programmer at Overflux Studios, you will be responsible for designing, implementing, and maintaining software solutions that contribute to innovative and engaging gaming experiences. The ideal candidate should have a strong background in programming languages such as C++, Java, or Python and a passion for creating high-quality games.

Designer

Join our dynamic design team to shape the visual and artistic aspects of our games. As a Designer at Overflux Studios, you will work on creating visually stunning and immersive designs for our gaming products. The ideal candidate should have a keen eye for detail, proficiency in design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, and a passion for delivering exceptional user-centric designs in the gaming industry.

3D Modeler

We are looking for a skilled 3D Modeler to contribute to the development of realistic and captivating game environments and characters. As a 3D Modeler at Overflux Studios, you will be involved in creating 3D models, textures, and animations that enhance the overall gaming experience. The ideal candidate should have expertise in 3D modeling software, an eye for detail, and a passion for pushing the boundaries of visual storytelling in games.

Overflux Studios Policies

Equal Opportunity Employment Policy:

Overflux Studios is an equal opportunity employer. We are committed to providing employment opportunities to all individuals without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, or any other characteristic protected by applicable law. All employment decisions are based on merit, qualifications, and business needs.

Code of Conduct:

Our Code of Conduct serves as a set of binding rules outlining the ethical and professional behavior expected of all employees at Overflux Studios. Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

Anti-Harassment and Anti-Discrimination Policy:

Overflux Studios strictly prohibits any form of harassment or discrimination. This includes unwelcome conduct based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, or any other protected characteristic. Employees found to be in violation of this policy will be subject to appropriate disciplinary measures, including termination.

Workplace Health and Safety Policy:

Our Workplace Health and Safety Policy outlines the legal obligations of Overflux Studios to provide a safe and healthy work environment. Employees are expected to comply with safety regulations and report any unsafe conditions promptly. Failure to adhere to safety standards may result in disciplinary action.

Remote Work Policy:

This policy governs the legal aspects of remote work arrangements, including the expectation of employees to maintain confidentiality, cybersecurity requirements, and compliance with all relevant laws and regulations. Violation of this policy may result in the termination of remote work privileges.

Intellectual Property Policy:

Employees are required to adhere to our Intellectual Property Policy, which clearly defines the ownership and protection of intellectual property. Violation of this policy may result in legal action to protect the company's proprietary information.

Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement:

All employees must sign a Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement, legally binding them to protect confidential information, trade secrets, and proprietary data belonging to Overflux Studios. Breach of this agreement may result in legal action.

Performance Evaluation and Feedback Policy:

This policy establishes the legal framework for performance evaluations, feedback mechanisms, and professional development opportunities. Decisions related to employment, promotions, and terminations will be based on documented performance evaluations.

Social Media and Communications Policy:

Employees are legally bound by our Social Media and Communications Policy, which outlines the responsible use of social media and communication channels. Breach of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

Data Protection and Privacy Policy:

Our Data Protection and Privacy Policy aligns with relevant data protection laws. Employees must comply with data processing and protection requirements, and violations may result in legal consequences.

Whistleblower Policy:

Overflux Studios encourages employees to report any unethical or illegal conduct through our Whistleblower Policy. Legal protections are in place for whistleblowers, and the company is committed to investigating reports promptly.
